By downloading and using our apps, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and you expressly consent with the collection, use and disclosure of your information. You are always free to opt out of the information collection by not using our mobile applications or services.

KIDS APPS PRIVACY POLICY

This Kids Apps Privacy Policy is appointed to provide the legal guardian of a child under the age of 13 with guidance regarding our privacy policies that concern collecting, using and disclosing personal information of children under 13.

Parents and legal guardians of children under 13 have certain rights under Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA), and Kambrium Games recognizes those rights.

Kambrium Games does not knowingly and directly collect and store any personal information from children under the age 13 as that would be a violation of COPPA. Some information may be collected by third party providers of services (advertising networks, etc.) or certain products.

In order to know if the user is a child according to the legislation in the user’s jurisdiction, the Kids App will present an age screen on launch. Based on the provided age information and device settings, Kambrium Games does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the minimum age limit that is prescribed by the legislation in the individual jurisdictions.

Kambrium Games does not allow children to send us any personal information, including your name, address, telephone number, email address or any other personal information. If such personal information has been processed without Kambrium Games’ knowledge, we will, after having become aware of the incident, immediately take reasonable measures to stop such processing and promptly delete any such information from the records.

If you believe that Kambrium Games might have any information from or about a child, or have any questions about privacy practices related to children under the applicable minimum age, please contact us at: contact@kambriumgames.com.
